# Redwood Mobility Brand Guidelines
## Logo: Broken Orbit

---

## Logo Concept

The Broken Orbit mark evolves from the original tree ring concept, transforming static concentric circles into dynamic, offset rings with strategic gaps. This creates a sense of forward momentum and innovation while maintaining the connection to redwood heritageâ€”growth rings that tell a story of time and permanence.

The center point anchors the mark, representing the core of the business, while the orbiting rings suggest expansion, movement, and the networks Redwood Mobility builds.

## Logo Versions

### Primary (Full Color)
- **Light backgrounds:** Copper rings with Forest Green center dot
- **Dark backgrounds:** Copper rings with Light Sand center dot

### Single Color
- **Copper:** Use when printing in one color on light backgrounds
- **White:** Use when printing in one color on dark backgrounds

### Lockups
- **Horizontal:** Logo mark + wordmark side by side (preferred for headers, email signatures)
- **Stacked:** Logo mark above wordmark (preferred for square formats, social media)

---

## Logo Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the diameter of the center dot on all sides. This ensures the logo has room to breathe and maintains visual impact.

---

## Minimum Size

- **Logo mark only:** 24px minimum (digital), 0.25" minimum (print)
- **Horizontal lockup:** 120px minimum width (digital), 1.25" minimum (print)
- **Stacked lockup:** 80px minimum width (digital), 0.75" minimum (print)

---

## Typography

**Wordmark Font:** System UI / San Francisco / Segoe UI (weight: 800/Extra Bold)
**Letter Spacing:** 0.12em (horizontal) / 0.18em (stacked)

For body copy and marketing materials, use a clean sans-serif such as:
- Inter
- SF Pro
- Helvetica Neue

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
- Use approved color combinations
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Use the appropriate version for background color
- Scale proportionally

### Don't:
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the colors outside brand palette
- Add effects (drop shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Rotate the logo
- Place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
